Whispers of Wonder

"Whispers of Wonder" is an enchanting interactive storytelling experience that combines artificial intelligence with Ghibli-style artistry. This magical adventure lets you explore three distinct realms - a mystical forest, an ancient desert canyon, and a winter wonderland - where your choices shape the narrative. Powered by advanced AI technologies including GPT-4 and RAG, along with Ghibli-inspired image generation, each journey creates a unique blend of visual and narrative magic that responds to your decisions and actions.

Milestone 1: Interactive Adventure Game with AI

Begin your magical journey with our AI-powered interactive storytelling experience. This milestone creates an immersive Ghibli-style adventure where your choices shape the narrative.

  • Dynamic Storytelling: Powered by GPT-4, creating responsive and engaging narrative based on user inputs.
  • Visual Generation: Utilizes Hugging Face's Flux Image Genaration model to generate scene illustrations that match the story.
  • Real-time Interaction: Seamless AJAX-based interface allowing continuous story progression without page reloads.
  • Framework: Built with Flask backend and jQuery frontend, featuring responsive design and loading status.

Milestone 2: Interactive Adventure with RAG Enhancement

Experience an enchanted journey through three magical realms, where LangChain and RAG technology combine to create rich, contextually-aware narratives that respond to your choices.

  • Magical Environments: Choose from three distinct scenarios - Mystic Forest, Desert Canyon, or Snowy Adventure, each with unique visuals and storytelling elements.
  • Enhanced Storytelling: Powered by LangChain and RAG technology, creating deeper narratives by drawing from a curated database of adventure and fantasy stories.
  • Dynamic Visuals: Ghibli-style artwork generation that adapts to your actions, complemented by interactive particle effects that match each environment.
  • Responsive Design: Immersive interface with scenario-specific color themes, smooth animations, and real-time visual feedback.
